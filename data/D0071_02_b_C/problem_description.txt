The power station in Sikkim needs to plan monthly power generation to meet consumption needs, utilizing historical daily power consumption records.
The decision variable is the daily power generation quantity, defined for next month in the planning horizon.
The objective is to minimize total operating costs, including a linear generation cost coefficient of 5 per unit and a shortage penalty coefficient of 10 per unit, summed over all days.
The total generation quantity for a month must not exceed the maximum capacity limit of 100 units.
The actual monthly power consumption is uncertain.
