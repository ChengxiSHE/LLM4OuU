The power station must determine daily power generation levels to meet uncertain consumption demands while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation amount, which must be planned for the next 30 days.
The objective is to minimize total cost comprising generation cost (weight 1) and excess power penalty cost (weight 0.5), forming a linear function.
Daily generation must not exceed the maximum capacity of 40 MWh.
The actual daily power consumption is uncertain based on historical data patterns.
