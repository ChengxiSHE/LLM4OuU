The company Yulu needs to optimize the daily rental bike operations in India, where the demand for rented bikes varies each day based on historical data.
The decision variable is the number of bikes to allocate for rent each workday.
The objective is to minimize the total operational cost, which includes holding costs and shortage cost, both with equal weight coefficients of 0.5 in the objective function.
The number of rented bikes each day should be a non-negative integer.
The daily rental demand is uncertain, based on historical rental data, which reflects variability in customer demand.
