The power station must decide the daily power generation amount to meet uncertain consumption demand while minimizing operational costs.
The decision variable is the daily power generation quantity allocated for the next 30 days.
The objective is to minimize total cost over the period, comprising linear generation cost (weight=1) and linear penalty cost for deviation from actual consumption (weight=0.5).
The daily power generation quantity must be non-negative.
The actual daily power consumption is the uncertain parameter.
