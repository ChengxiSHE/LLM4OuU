The company needs to determine daily procurement quantities for a supply chain product to manage price volatility. The decision variable is the daily procurement quantity, planned for the next 30 days. The objective is to maximize the total expected revenue minus the total procurement cost, where revenue equals procurement quantity multiplied by the constant selling price of $0.2 per unit, and cost equals procurement quantity multiplied by the uncertain daily unit price. The daily procurement quantity must not exceed 1000 units due to storage limitations. The daily unit price of the product is uncertain, based on historical data.
