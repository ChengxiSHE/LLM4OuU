The health department needs to allocate medical resources for treating male COVID-19 patients based on the number of new confirmed cases. 
The decision variable is the daily quantity of medical resource units allocated for male patients over the next 30 days. 
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising resource allocation cost (50 currency units per unit) and shortage penalty cost (200 currency units per shortage unit). 
The daily resource allocation must satisfy: minimum of 10 units and maximum of 50 units. 
The daily number of new confirmed male COVID-19 patients is uncertain, based on historical case data.
