The power station needs to determine daily power generation levels to meet uncertain consumption demands while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation quantity, planned for the next 30 days.
The objective is to minimize the total cost over 30 days, comprising a generation cost coefficient of 0.8 and a shortage penalty coefficient of 1.2, both linear in the decision variable.
The daily generation quantity must be non-negative.
The daily power consumption is uncertain, based on historical data patterns.
