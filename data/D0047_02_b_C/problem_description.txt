The power plant must plan daily electricity generation to meet uncertain demand while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily electricity generation amount of the upcoming month, which must be determined for each day.
The objective is to minimize total cost, comprising generation cost with a coefficient of 2 and shortage penalty with a coefficient of 5.
Daily generation must not exceed the plant's maximum capacity of 220 MWh.
The daily power consumption demand is uncertain and derived from historical data.
