The health department needs to allocate medical resources for treating female COVID-19 patients based on the number of new confirmed cases. 
The decision variable is the daily quantity of medical resource units allocated for female patients over the next 30 days. 
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising resource allocation cost (60 currency units per unit) and shortage penalty cost (250 currency units per shortage unit). 
The daily resource allocation must satisfy: minimum of 15 units and maximum of 60 units. 
The daily number of new confirmed female COVID-19 patients is uncertain, based on historical case data.
