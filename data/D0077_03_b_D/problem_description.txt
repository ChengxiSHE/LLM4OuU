A distributor must determine weekly salt purchase quantities to maximize profitability. The decision variable is the weekly salt purchase quantity, planned for the upcoming 4 weeks. The objective is to maximize total expected profit, where profit equals total revenue minus total cost, with revenue calculated as purchase quantity multiplied by a constant selling price of $120 per unit, and total cost comprising purchase cost (purchase quantity multiplied by the uncertain weekly average salt price with a weight of 1) and holding cost (purchase quantity multiplied by a holding cost coefficient of 0.5). The weekly purchase quantity must be non-negative and must not exceed 1000 units due to storage limitations. The weekly average salt price is uncertain and influences the purchase cost directly.
