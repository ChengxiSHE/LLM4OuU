The Florida civil affairs department needs to plan weekly funeral service allocation based on COVID-19 mortality trends for the next 52 weeks. 
The decision variable is the weekly number of funeral service capacity units to allocate. 
The objective is to minimize the total operational cost, comprising service operational cost (180 currency units per unit) and capacity insufficiency penalty cost (4500 currency units per unserved death case). 
The weekly service allocation must satisfy: minimum of 3500 units and maximum of 4300 units. 
The weekly observed COVID-19 death count is uncertain, based on historical mortality data.
