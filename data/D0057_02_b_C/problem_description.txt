The power station must plan its daily power generation to meet uncertain consumption demands while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation, planned for each day of the month.
The objective is to minimize total cost, comprising a generation cost coefficient of 50 $/MWh and a penalty coefficient of 80 $/MWh for any unmet demand.
Daily power generation must be non-negative and not exceed the maximum capacity of 90 MWh.
