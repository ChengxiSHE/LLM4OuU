 A supermarket chain must determine weekly pricing strategies for consumer goods in Colombo to maximize revenue while considering fluctuating market conditions. The decision variable is the weekly selling price set for the goods, planned over a four-week period. The objective is to maximize total expected revenue, modeled as a linear function where each week’s revenue equals the product of the selling price and a weighting coefficient of 1.
The weekly selling price must remain within a predefined range, such as between a minimum and maximum price limit. The weekly retail price of goods, which influences revenue, is uncertain and derived from historical data.
