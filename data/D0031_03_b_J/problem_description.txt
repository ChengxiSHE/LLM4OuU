The hospital must determine daily supply chain budgets to manage operational costs while accommodating expenditure fluctuations. The decision variable is the daily budget allocation amount, planned for the upcoming 30 days. The objective is to minimize total deviation costs between actual and budgeted expenditures, with equal over- and under-budget penalty coefficients of 1. The daily budget must be non-negative to ensure feasible financial planning. The actual daily supply chain expenditure is uncertain.
