The power plant must plan daily power generation to meet uncertain consumption demands while minimizing operational costs. The decision variable is the daily power generation amount in megawatts, planned for each day of the upcoming month. The objective is to minimize total cost, which equals the sum of generation cost (with a coefficient of 50 $/MWh) and penalty cost for unmet demand (with a coefficient of 100 $/MWh). The daily power generation must be non-negative and not exceed the plant's maximum capacity of 500 MW. The daily power consumption is uncertain, based on historical data.
