When treating patients with cardiovascular disease, doctors need to decide on the injection dose of quinidine at each moment, and the registered dose depends on the patient's serum quinidine concentration at each hour. I hope your decision can minimize the comprehensive cost of dose deviation from the ideal value, including efficacy deviation cost and drug side effect cost, and both have the same proportion in the objective function, that is, the weight coefficients are both 1. The decision variable is the injection dose of quinidine each hour for the next day. The constraint condition is that the injection dose at each hour is non negative. The serum quinidine concentration of patients each hour is unknown.
