The power station must determine its daily power generation to meet uncertain demand while minimizing costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation level, planned for the next 30 days.
The objective is to minimize total cost, with a linear function combining generation cost (weight 5) and shortage penalty (weight 2).
Daily generation must be non-negative and not exceed the station's maximum capacity of 10 MWh.
The daily power consumption demand is uncertain, based on historical data.
