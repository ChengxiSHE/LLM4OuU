The power plant needs to plan daily power generation to meet uncertain consumption demands while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation quantity in the upcoming month.  
The objective is to minimize total generation cost, with linear generation cost (weight 3) and shortage penalty (weight 2).  
Daily generation must be non-negative and cannot exceed the plant's maximum capacity of 300 MWh.  
The daily power consumption is uncertain and derived from historical data patterns.
