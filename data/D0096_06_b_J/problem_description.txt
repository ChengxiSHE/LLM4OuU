The civil affairs department needs to plan weekly funeral service allocation for Alabama for the next 52 weeks. 
The decision variable is the weekly number of funeral service capacity units to allocate. 
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising service operational cost (150 currency units per unit) and capacity insufficiency penalty cost (4000 currency units per unserved death case). 
The weekly service allocation must satisfy: minimum of 800 units and maximum of 1000 units. 
The weekly observed death count is uncertain, based on historical CDC excess mortality data.
