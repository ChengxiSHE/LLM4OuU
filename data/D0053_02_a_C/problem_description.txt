The power station must plan daily power generation to meet uncertain consumption demands while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation amount, planned for each day of the next month.
The objective is to minimize the total cost of generation and potential shortfall, with generation cost coefficient 0.8 and shortfall penalty coefficient 1.2.
The daily generation must at least meet a minimum safety level of 140 MWh.
The daily power consumption is uncertain, based on historical data patterns.
