A company needs to determine monthly olive oil purchase quantities for the next year, balancing procurement costs against market price volatility. The decision variable is the monthly olive oil purchase quantity, planned for each of the next 12 months. The objective is to maximize total expected profit, where profit equals total revenue minus total procurement cost, with revenue calculated as purchase quantity multiplied by a constant selling price of $12 per unit, and cost equals purchase quantity multiplied by uncertain olive oil prices. Each monthly purchase quantity must be non-negative and must not exceed 1000 units due to storage limitations. The monthly olive oil price is uncertain, based on historical market data.
