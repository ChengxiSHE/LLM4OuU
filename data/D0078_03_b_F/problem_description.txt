A fried chicken restaurant needs to determine the weekly chicken purchase quantity to maximize profitability. The decision variable is the weekly chicken purchase quantity, and it is planned for each week within a 4-week period. The objective is to maximize the total expected profit, where profit equals total revenue minus total procurement cost, with revenue calculated as purchase quantity multiplied by a constant selling price of $1600 per unit, and cost equals purchase quantity multiplied by the uncertain chicken price (with a weight coefficient of 1). The weekly purchase quantity must be non-negative and must not exceed 1000 units due to storage limitations. The weekly chicken price is uncertain and is derived from historical data rather than prediction.
