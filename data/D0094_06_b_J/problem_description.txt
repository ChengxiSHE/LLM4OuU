The public health department needs to plan weekly chickenpox vaccine allocation for Csongrad for the next 52 weeks. 
The decision variable is the weekly number of vaccine doses to allocate. 
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising vaccine procurement and administration cost (30 currency units per dose) and outbreak penalty cost (200 currency units per unprevented case). 
The weekly vaccine allocation must satisfy: minimum of 50 doses and maximum of 120 doses. 
The actual weekly chickenpox case count is uncertain, based on historical outbreak data.
