The power station needs to plan daily power generation to meet uncertain consumption demands while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation amount planned for each day of the upcoming month.
The objective is to minimize total generation cost, with a linear cost coefficient of 0.5 per MWh generated, plus a penalty coefficient of 1.0 per MWh of unmet demand.
The daily generation must be non-negative and not exceed the station's maximum capacity of 10 MWh per day.
The daily power consumption is uncertain, based on historical data patterns.
