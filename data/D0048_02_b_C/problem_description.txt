The power plant needs to determine daily power generation levels to meet uncertain consumption demands while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation quantity, which must be planned for the next 30 days.
The objective is to minimize total generation cost, comprising generation cost with a coefficient of 3 and shortage penalty with a coefficient of 5.
The daily generation quantity must be non-negative.
The daily power consumption is uncertain based on historical data patterns.
