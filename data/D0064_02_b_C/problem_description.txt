The power station must plan daily power generation to meet uncertain demand while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation amount (in MWh), planned for each day of the upcoming month.
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising a generation cost coefficient of 50 $/MWh and a penalty coefficient of 100 $/MWh for any unmet demand.
Daily generation must not exceed the maximum capacity of 120 MWh.
The daily power consumption is the uncertain parameter.
