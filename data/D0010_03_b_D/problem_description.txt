The supermarket needs to determine daily product order quantities to balance inventory costs against potential lost sales from stockouts. The decision variable is the daily order quantity for products, planned for the upcoming 30 days. The objective is to minimize total daily costs, comprising holding cost with weight 0.7 and shortage cost with weight 0.3, both linear in the order quantity. The daily order quantity must be non-negative to ensure operational feasibility. The daily sales volume is uncertain, based on historical data patterns.
