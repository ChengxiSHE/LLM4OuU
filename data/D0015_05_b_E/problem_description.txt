The city of Seoul needs to optimize the daily rental bike operations, where the demand for rented bikes varies each day based on historical data.
The decision variable is the number of bikes to allocate for rent each day.
The objective is to minimize the total operational cost, which includes shortage and overflow costs, both with equal weight coefficients of 0.5 in the objective function.
The number of rented bikes each day should be a non-negative integer.
The daily rental demand is uncertain, based on historical rental data, which reflects variability in customer demand.
