Many cities have introduced rental bicycles, and it is very important to provide and use rental bicycles to the public at the right time. A bike sharing company needs to decide on the number of bicycles to be deployed at specific stations per hour. Excessive deployment can result in wastage and loss of bicycles, while insufficient deployment can lead to loss of customers. Therefore, the company's advertising strategy needs to minimize two types of losses as much as possible,  and both have the same proportion in the objective function, with a weight coefficient of 1. Meanwhile, the quantity of bicycle demand is unknown.
