The power plant needs to determine daily power generation to meet consumption demand while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation amount for the next 30 days.
The objective is to minimize total generation cost, comprising generation cost (weight 1) and shortage penalty (weight 2), both linear in generation level.
The daily generation must at least equal the plant's minimum consumption of 50 MWH to prevent shortages.
The daily power consumption is uncertain, derived from historical data patterns.
