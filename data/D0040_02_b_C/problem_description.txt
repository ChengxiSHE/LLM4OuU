The power plant needs to plan its power generation per 10 minutes to meet uncertain consumption levels.
The decision variable is the planned power generation level for every 10 minutes for the next hour.
The objective is to minimize the total cost of power generation and potential shortages, with generation cost weighted by 0.8 and shortage cost by 0.2.
The generation level per 10 minutes must be non-negative.
The power consumption per 10 minutes is uncertain, based on historical patterns.
