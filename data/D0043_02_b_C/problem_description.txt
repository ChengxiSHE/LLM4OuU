The power plant must plan daily power generation to meet uncertain demand while minimizing operational costs.  
The decision variable is the daily power generation amount, planned for each day of the upcoming month.
The objective is to minimize total generation cost, with overuse weighted at 2 and underuse penalties weighted at 3.
Daily generation must be at least the historical minimum consumption of 1.6 to ensure basic supply reliability.
The actual daily power consumption is uncertain, based on historical data patterns.
