The company must determine weekly coconut oil procurement quantities to maximize profitability. The decision variable is the weekly coconut oil procurement quantity, planned for the upcoming 4 weeks. The objective is to maximize total expected profit, where profit equals total revenue minus total procurement cost, with revenue calculated as procurement quantity multiplied by a constant selling price of $550 per unit, and cost equals procurement quantity multiplied by the uncertain weekly coconut oil price. The weekly procurement quantity must be non-negative and must not exceed 1000 units due to storage limitations. The weekly coconut oil price is uncertain and directly influences the procurement cost in the objective function.
