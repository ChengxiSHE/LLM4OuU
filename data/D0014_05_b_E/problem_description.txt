The shared car companies are responsible for the operation of shared car charging piles every day. I hope your decision can enable the company to meet the charging demand with the lowest possible power reserve cost, where the power reserve cost is the power holding cost and the power shortage cost, and both have the same weight coefficient in the objective function, which is 1. The decision variable is the number of amount of electricity reserved for the independent N shared car charging piles for the next day. The charging demand for the next day is unknown.
