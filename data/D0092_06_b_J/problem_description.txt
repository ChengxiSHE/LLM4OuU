The hospital needs to plan daily patient admission capacity for the next 30 days. 
The decision variable is the daily number of patients to admit. 
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising fixed operational cost (50 currency units per admission slot) and penalty cost for insufficient capacity (1000 currency units per patient beyond capacity). 
The daily admission capacity must satisfy: minimum of 50 patients and maximum of 500 patients. 
The daily number of arriving patients is uncertain, based on historical operational data.
