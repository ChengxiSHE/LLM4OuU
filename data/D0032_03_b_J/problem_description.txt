The hospital must determine daily medical resource procurement quantities to meet fluctuating patient demands while minimizing costs. The decision variable is the daily procurement quantity of the medical resource, planned for the next 30 days. The objective is to minimize total cost, comprising procurement cost with weight 1 and shortage penalty with weight 2, both linear in the decision variable. The daily procurement quantity must be non-negative to ensure operational feasibility. The daily minimum demand for the medical resource is uncertain, based on historical data.
