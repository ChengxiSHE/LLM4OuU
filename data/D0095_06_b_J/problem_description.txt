The public health department needs to plan weekly resource allocation for chickenpox outbreaks in Zala for the next 52 weeks. 
The decision variable is the weekly number of medical resource units to allocate. 
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising resource deployment cost (10 currency units per unit) and prevention insufficiency penalty cost (100 currency units per case). 
The weekly resource allocation must satisfy: minimum of 20 units and maximum of 70 units. 
The actual weekly chickenpox case count is uncertain, based on historical outbreak data.
