A company must determine daily procurement quantities for a supply chain product to maximize profitability. The decision variable is the daily procurement quantity, planned for each day over the next 30 days. The objective is to maximize the total expected revenue minus the total procurement cost, where revenue equals procurement quantity multiplied by a constant selling price of $0.2 per unit, and cost equals procurement quantity multiplied by the uncertain daily lowest price. The daily procurement quantity must be non-negative and must not exceed 1000 units due to storage limitations. The daily lowest price of the product is uncertain and directly influences the procurement cost in the objective function.
