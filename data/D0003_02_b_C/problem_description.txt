The production company needs to decide on the daily power configuration for the coming month, and the specific number of configurations depends on the daily power demand. I hope your decision can minimize the total cost of the production company, including distribution costs, waste caused by electricity redundancy, and losses caused by power shortages, and that all three have the same proportion in the objective function, with weight coefficients of 1. The decision variable is the daily power configuration for the coming month.  The daily power configuration must be non-negative. The daily electricity demand is unknown.
