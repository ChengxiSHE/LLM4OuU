The retail company needs to determine daily inventory levels to balance holding costs against potential lost sales from stockouts. The decision variable is the daily inventory quantity planned for the next 30 days. The objective is to minimize total cost over 30 days, where holding cost per unit has weight 0.5 and shortage cost per unit has weight 1 in the linear objective function. The daily inventory quantity must be non-negative. The daily sales volume is uncertain based on historical data.
