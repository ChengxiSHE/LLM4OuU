A solar cell system is installed in a household. A corresponding amount of electricity needs to be reserved in the solar cell system every day. I hope your decision can enable the family to meet the electricity demand with the lowest possible reserved electricity cost, where the reserved electricity cost is the electricity holding cost and the power shortage cost, and both have the same weight coefficient in the objective function, which is 1. The decision variable is the reserved electrical energy of each households for next day. The electricity consumption of a household is unknown.
