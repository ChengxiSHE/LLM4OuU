Health authorities need to plan the allocation of COVID-19 testing resources for the next 30 days.
The decision variable is the daily number of test kits to allocate.
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising testing cost (50 currency units per test kit) and penalty cost for undetected cases (1000 currency units per undetected case).
The daily testing allocation must satisfy: minimum of 100 test kits and maximum of 800 test kits.
The number of new confirmed COVID-19 cases each day is uncertain, based on historical data distribution.
