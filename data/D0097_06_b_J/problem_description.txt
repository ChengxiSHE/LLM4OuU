The civil affairs department needs to plan weekly funeral service allocation for California for the next 52 weeks. 
The decision variable is the weekly number of funeral service capacity units to allocate. 
The objective is to minimize the total cost, comprising service operational cost (120 currency units per unit) and capacity insufficiency penalty cost (5000 currency units per unserved death case). 
The weekly service allocation must satisfy: minimum of 4000 units and maximum of 5500 units. 
The weekly observed COVID-19 death count is uncertain, based on historical mortality data.
